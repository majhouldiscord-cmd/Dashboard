Privacy Policy

Last updated November 3, 2025

To provide our services, we collect and process certain data from our users. We only collect data that is necessary for the operation and improvement of our services. By using our services, you confirm that you have read and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

1. COLLECTED DATA

To provide our services, we collect and store certain user and server data provided by Discord Inc. This may include your Discord User ID, name, username, and avatar, as well as your server ID and channel IDs.

Additionally, we may store data necessary for the operation of our features, such as webhook information or uploaded files. In certain cases (e.g., surveys or abuse prevention), we may store your IP address in an anonymized form. This data is not linked to your Discord profile and is used solely for misuse prevention and fraud protection.

2. AI DATA PROCESSING

Some features of our service use artificial intelligence (AI) technologies provided by third parties. These technologies may include, but are not limited to, Google's Generative AI platform (Gemini API) and large language models accessed via Groq (e.g. LLaMA-based models), as well as other comparable AI services that may be introduced or replaced over time. When you use AI-powered features, your input (such as text, messages, or uploaded content) may be transmitted to one or more of these providers for processing in order to generate responses.

According to Google's AI Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy, data sent to Google's AI systems may be used to provide, maintain, and improve Google's products and machine learning models. This applies to most Gemini API usage, unless the developer uses a paid enterprise plan with data-use restrictions or an explicit opt-out. Data processed by other AI providers (such as Groq) is subject to their respective terms of service and privacy policies.

We strongly advise you not to include personal, confidential, or sensitive information when using AI-powered features. By using these features, you consent to the processing of your input data by Google, Groq, and other third-party AI service providers as described above and in our Privacy Policy.

3. TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES AND COOKIES

Cookies or Browser Cookies. A cookie is a small file placed on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some parts of our service may not function properly.

Web Beacons. Certain sections of our Service and our emails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that allow us, for example, to count users who have visited specific pages or opened an email, and to gather related website statistics (such as page popularity and system integrity verification).

Google Analytics. This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies to help analyze how users use the website. The information generated by the cookie about your use of this website (including your IP address) is transmitted to and stored on servers operated by Google in the United States. Google may use this information to evaluate website use, compile reports, and provide other analytics services. You can disable cookies in your browser settings to prevent tracking, but some functionality may be affected.

4. DATA PROTECTION

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our site complies with the data protection requirements of the European Union (GDPR).

5. USER RESPONSIBILITY

Users are responsible for the content they submit and should not share any personal, confidential, or inappropriate information through our services. Please avoid submitting information you would not want to become public or used for AI model improvement.

6. MODIFICATION RIGHTS

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. DATA DELETION

Every user has the right to request deletion of their stored data at any time. Depending on the type of data, it may take up to 30 days to process deletion requests. Please contact us directly if you wish to have your data removed.
