Guidelines

Guidelines

Last updated November 3, 2025

Dead Chat Reviver ("DCR") was created to make Discord servers more active and enjoyable — not for spam or abuse. These guidelines exist to ensure that everyone can use the bot responsibly and fairly.

As a user, you probably don't want to get spammed or harassed, and neither does anyone else. By following these rules, you help make DCR a fun and positive experience for everyone.

These guidelines apply to all users, servers, and uses of DCR, including messages, automation settings, AI-generated content, and any behavior related to the bot.

If we determine that someone is violating these guidelines, we may take appropriate action, which may include: warnings, removing content, blacklisting (banning) servers or users, or reporting serious cases to Discord or law enforcement.

If you encounter a user, message, or server that violates these guidelines, please report it to us via admin@dcr.poldisweb.de.

1. SPAM

DCR is not intended for spam — it's meant to make your community more active in a positive way.

The most common complaints we receive are from users being spammed with repeated or unwanted messages. Spam negatively impacts communities and violates both our rules and Discord's.

We do not tolerate spam. Any user or server found to be using the bot for spam purposes may be blacklisted (banned) from DCR.

DCR includes automated detection for spam behavior. Repeated spam or automated abuse will trigger immediate enforcement actions.

If you need to adjust message frequency or limits, please visit our help center for legitimate ways to increase limits safely.

2. MISUSE

Misuse of DCR includes using the bot or its features for any of the following:

  • spreading false or misleading information
  • organizing, promoting, or participating in harassment or hate speech
  • making threats of violence or encouraging harm to others
  • sexualizing minors or sharing sexually explicit material
  • promoting or distributing adult or pornographic content
  • encouraging self-harm, suicide, or dangerous behavior
  • sharing violent, gory, or animal cruelty content
  • violating copyright, trademarks, or others' intellectual property rights
  • using the bot to break Discord's Terms of Service or Community Guidelines

We take these violations seriously. Servers or users engaging in any of the above may be permanently blacklisted from the service.

3. AI AND AUTOMATION

Some DCR features may use artificial intelligence (AI) or automated systems to generate responses or conversation starters. These systems may include, but are not limited to, Google's Gemini API and large language models provided via Groq (e.g. LLaMA-based models), as well as other comparable AI services that may be added or changed over time. These features are provided for fun, convenience, and engagement — not for abuse.

You may not use AI-generated outputs to:

  • spread hate, misinformation, or disinformation
  • generate or distribute harmful, offensive, or illegal content
  • impersonate real people or deliberately mislead users
  • collect, analyze, or share private or personal data from others

Please also avoid submitting sensitive, personal, or confidential data into AI-powered features. As noted in our Privacy Policy, such inputs may be processed by third-party AI providers (e.g. Google or Groq) in order to deliver the requested functionality and may be subject to their respective privacy policies and data processing practices.

4. VIOLATION OF TERMS OF SERVICE

Violating these Guidelines or our Terms of Service may result in temporary or permanent restrictions, including suspension or blacklisting of your server or account.

We may also report serious or repeated violations to Discord or law enforcement authorities when necessary.
